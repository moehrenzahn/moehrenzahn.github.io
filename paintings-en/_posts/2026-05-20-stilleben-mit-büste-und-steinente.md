My dear mother-in-law has this amazing talent for decoration, so I never have to arrange my own still lifes — I just have to visit her, and every time there’s something new to discover.

A few years ago, on the same windowsill, I discovered [this cool cheese shoe](/en/paintings/2023-04-29-stillleben-mit-b%C3%BCste-und-k%C3%A4seschuh/).

This time, I’ve fallen for this cute little duck (or whatever kind of animal it’s supposed to be) made of black stone.
