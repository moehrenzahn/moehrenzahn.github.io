Hi, I'm Max, the app developer behind Flyleaf. I'm only working on Flyleaf on nights and weekends, but I love to hear from Flyleaf users and aim to reply to every email (eventually). If you ever run into issues using Flyleaf or have a question not answered [in the FAQ](/project/flyleaf/faq/), please don't hesitate so end me an email:

> [Send a support email to Max](mailto:flyleaf@moehrenzahn.de)
